Background

Background

There is a very serious problem of verifying people, businesses as well as institutions in Africa.

This is backed by the fact that no standard uniform address system is prevalent in the region that is supported by its local institutions.

Businesses and Investors struggle with fulfilling KYC requirements, which differ country to country, institution to institution, as well as with regulators.

The evolving world of investment and changing dynamics in Africa means it’s the most sought after for opportunities, but lack of confidence and trust in identifying or reviewing partners/potential partners is killing the many opportunities that exists.

Real Life Experiences of the Problem by founder:

After successfully setting up a financial institution that operated in multiple countries in Africa, he faced an uphill challenge in fulfilling KYC requirements for ready-made clients that struggled to be converted to active clients due to challenges faced in fulfilling POA (Proof of Address) requirements.

The Founder travelled across 23 countries in Africa over 10 years in quest of finding a solution since he has been on both sides of the challenge.

This led to the creation of an integrated system that verifies and connects businesses, individuals and entrepreneurs. This business also creates an ecosystem where all parties communicate and interact confidently with each other.

The rating and verification system is based on various factors such as individual/business history, financial stability, and legal compliance.

The platform provides investors with a reliable source of information, which will increase their confidence in African businesses and industries.

Our platform benefits investors (Seekers) by reducing risks and increasing their confidence in potential business partnerships.

At the same time, it will also benefit individuals and businesses (Providers) operating in Africa by providing them with a platform to showcase their credibility and reliability.
